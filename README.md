# Lejiaohao Future Studies

Lejiaohao Future Studies (乐郊号未来学调研).

Essays and surveys on long-term futures under known physical constraints.

This repository is for future-studies writing, astroengineering analysis, civilization-scale reasoning, and public explanatory essays.

[Work in progress. More content coming soon. Stay tuned.]

## Scope

Possible topics include:

- Civilization-level evolution.
- Astroengineering and megastructures.
- Stellar engineering.
- Post-biological civilization.
- Long-term computation.
- Energy and material flows.
- Governance under advanced technological capacity.

## Method

The repository aims to distinguish:

- Established scientific or engineering knowledge.
- Quantitative estimates and order-of-magnitude reasoning.
- Plausible extrapolation.
- Open uncertainty.
